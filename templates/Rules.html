Instructions

This game requires you to bet on which side of a coin (heads or tails) you believe will appear next.

Unlike a normal coin which has a 50% chance of landing heads and a 50% chance of landing tails, the coin in this game is biased to land on heads 60% of the time and on tails 40% of the time.

In other words, the probability that the coin lands on heads is 0.6 and the probability that it lands on tails is 0.4. These probabilities will remain unchanged throughout the whole game.

We ask you to please base your decisions on these probabilities.

To begin with, we have placed £20 in an account for you to play with. Throughout this game we refer to this amount as your capital.

The aim of the game is to earn as much capital as possible.

This game will end in one of two ways: either you play all 60 rounds or you go bankrupt. Bankruptcy is when your current capital falls below £1.

Please remember the probability of the coin landing heads is 0.6 and the probability of it landing tails is 0.4. These probabilities will remain unchanged throughout both games.
